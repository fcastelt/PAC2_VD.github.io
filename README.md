## Flow Map
### Ukrainian Refugees Migration Map

This visualization illustrates the flow of Ukrainian refugees from Ukraine to European countries since the full-scale invasion of Ukraine on the 22nd of February. Data was collected on the 15th of April 2024.

Explore the interactive representation [here](https://app.flowmap.city/public/5cb30e27-1ffd-4bff-8b4a-fe588a95a047).

![FlowMap](/FlowMap.png)

- **Data:** (/data/ukranian_refugees_data)
- **Source:** Office of the High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR). [Link](https://data.unhcr.org/en/situations/ukraine)
